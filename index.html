Welcome to Heroine's Duel!

Welcome to "Heroine's Duel," where the strongest and most skilled female heroes from anime face off in a high-stakes battle of wits and strategy! This game reinvents the classic Rock-Paper-Scissors format, spotlighting iconic heroines known for their extraordinary abilities and unique fighting styles.

Choose Your Champion:

Select from an array of powerful female characters, each embodying one of three distinct battle attributes: brute strength, strategic brilliance, or unmatched speed. Your choice will shape the outcome of every duel and test your tactical prowess.

Game Mechanics:

  • Rock (Strength): Unleash the raw power of legendary fighters like Erza Scarlet and Sakura Haruno, whose immense strength can crush any opponent who dares to stand in their way.
  • Paper (Strategy): Outmaneuver and outsmart your rivals with the tactical brilliance of characters like Bulma and Kaguya Shinomiya, who use their intellect and strategy to gain the upper hand.
